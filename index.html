🎓 What is Opportunity Cost?

Opportunity Cost is the value of the next best alternative that you give up when you make a choice.

If you chose the iPad, your opportunity cost was the potential $2,700 profit from investing in Apple stock.

If you chose the stock, your opportunity cost was the immediate satisfaction of having a new iPad, but you gained much more in the long run!

Key Lesson: Every choice has a cost. Smart investors think about the long-term consequences of their decisions.
